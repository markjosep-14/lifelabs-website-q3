Gender Stereotypes

Gender Stereotypes are negative as they restrict the abilities of both men and women to succeed in their career goals,
develop their personal skills, and make decisions in their lives. A gender stereotype is a broad assumption or idea regarding
attributes or characteristics, or responsibilities that are or should to be held by, or conducted by, men and women.
What is our Purpose?

Our purpose is to try as much as possible eliminate Gender Stereotyping and make it equal for both men and women.

We made an article to explain more in-depth things concerning on why Gender Stereotyping should not be tolerated.
